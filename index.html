Your Thanksgiving Simmer Pot Recipe

Fill your home with amazing aromas!

Thanksgiving is right around the corner so it is time to start thinking about meal prep!

Make sure all of your ingredients are organic and non-GMO. Ocean Spray cranberries are usually NOT organic.

In a large pot, add:

  • One big bag of cranberries
  • 2 sliced ginger roots with peel
  • 2 sliced lemons
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • A handful of fresh rosemary
  • A handful of fresh thyme
  • One tsp of cloves

Add water to cover and simmer on low heat for a couple of hours.

Add-ons:

  • Fresh oranges, blueberries and other fruits

Enjoy hot or cold!

Place in the fridge to enjoy the following days.
